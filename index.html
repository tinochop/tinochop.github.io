Tina Lee

I’m a writer, editor, and strategist in Brooklyn, NY.

I edit for A Book Apart — titles include Design for Real Life, On Web Typography, You’re My Favorite Client, Responsible Responsive Design, and Designing for Touch, among others.

In the past, I ran editorial at Tinybop, devised content plans and style guides at Mule Design, edited articles at A List Apart, and taught writing and sci-fi futures to undergrads. I received my MFA in Fiction from Syracuse University.

I’ve had the pleasure of working with these fine folks: 100Kin10 with Crush & Lovely, All Tomorrows, Bluecadet, Happy Cog, Matter, Medium, and Rookie (I edited Rookie Yearbook Four for print).

I’ve written on the importance of the sentence, dreamy games, and childhood mysteries. I also keep a journal, and enjoy dioramas. You can find me elsewhere as @tinochop and spacecreature.
