Tina Lee

I’m a writer, editor, and strategist in Brooklyn, NY.

I edit for A List Apart and A Book Apart — titles include On Web Typography, You’re My Favorite Client (2014), and others forthcoming.

In the past, I ran editorial at Tinybop, devised content plans and style guides at Mule Design, and taught writing and sci-fi futures to undergrads. I received my MFA in Fiction from Syracuse University.

I’ve written on the importance of the sentence, dreamy games, and childhood mysteries. I also enjoy dioramas. You can find me elsewhere as @tinochop and spacecreature.
